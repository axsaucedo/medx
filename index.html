An initiative like no other.

The MEDx Hackathon is an initiative supported by AMEE that strives to revolutionize ,medical education. A unique mission where 60 of the most brilliant developers, designers and entrepreneurs from the UK are gathered to tackle burning problems in medical education. With the help of health and industry professionals and mentors, our so called "health hackers" will convey concepts that will change health education for good.

Startups, apps, prototypes will be created, and presented in front of CEOs, investors, serial entrepreneurs and evangelists! With attendees from all over the UK, this hackathon will certainly promise a unique experience!

Crazy? Definitely! Impossible? Not even close! As with our past events, people will come up with revolutionary ideas that will change medical education! Some of our past initiatives where brilliant ideas where created include The #HackTheRails initiative, the Startup Bus UK 2014, the Startup Weekend Moscow, and Startup Hacks.
